Heyy folks!! This is HariPriya.

I'm honored you've taken the time to explore my work. I've worked at RedHat as a Technical Writer Intern and am currently part of the Google Season of Docs - NimbleEdge program.

A pre-final year student pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering from Dr B C Roy Engineering College, India majoring in Information Technology Branch. My experience consists of writing technical documentation and blogs.

There is nothing I love more than open source. And yes, of course! I have been able to take advantage of many opportunities because of open source. My first introduction to open source came from Fedora where I was part of Fedora Magazine. Later on, I led GirlScript Durgapur and the Google Developers Students Club for a year. Leading communities and meeting people, interacting with them, and discussing open source with them is my favorite part. Having volunteered for PyCon India 2021 and attended FoSSIndia 2022, I look forward to contributing in every possible way to more such communities.
